Controls

Mouse click
Moves the closest ship to the spot where mouse was clicked
Mouse drag
Drags the ship closest to the mouse clicked
W
Thrust ship(s) forward
S
Retro ship(s) backwards
A
Turn ship(s) left
D
Turn ship(s) right
<Space>
Fires a bullet from every ship
1
Generate a new standard ship at mousepoint
2
Generate a new modern ship at mousepoint
K
Kill ship closest to mouse
0
Toogles rendering of rocks ON/OFF
G
Turn gravity ON/OFF, bouncing the ships at top and bottom
M
Toggles mixed-keys input allowance ON/OFF
H
Halts the ship(s)
R
Resets the ship(s)
P
Pauses
O
Updates game by one frame
C
Toggles clearing of canvas ON/OFF
Q
Quits the game
T
Toggles timer ON/OFF
B
Toggles red box ON/OFF
U
Toggles white box ON/OFF (overlays the red box)
F
Toggles flipflop ON/OFF
